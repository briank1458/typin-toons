About

In each episode of the podcast, Kendall and a guest will discuss the guest’s journey to writing for animation and then take a deeper dive into a specific topic, such as: how to choreograph an action sequence, networking for writers, what to know before pitching to execs, how to address notes, etc. This is a podcast for those just starting out on their animation writing journey, those already on the long and winding path, and anyone interested in storytelling.


Kendall Michele Haney is an Emmy-nominated animation writer and story editor whose work consists of dozens of produced scripts, spanning multiple genres, including series for Disney, Netflix, Nickelodeon, and HBO Max. She is passionate about telling inclusive stories that inspire audiences to make positive changes in their world and believes the best way to do so is through the magic of the animation medium. Typin’ Toons is her attempt to take some of the mystery out of pursuing a career in writing for animation. Plus… she just loves talking about writing!
