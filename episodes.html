Ep 1 - Script Process with Nicole Dubuc
January 14, 2021
In the first official episode of the podcast, Kendall is joined by Nicole Dubuc (Kim Possible, Transformers: Rescue Bots, My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic, Star Wars Rebels, The Rocketeer) to talk about her script process. They discuss the importance of outlines, why it's sometimes best to step away and let an idea slow cook, and how to know when a script is finished.
Ep 0 - TV Animation Writing 101
January 14, 2021
In this "prologue" to the podcast, writers Isabel Galupo and Dan Salgarolo join Kendall to answer some of the most common questions we get about writing for TV animation. If you're brand new to animation writing or just starting out on your journey, this episode is for you!
